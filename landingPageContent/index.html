Pretzel Wheat

An interactive, web-based environment for navigating multi-dimensional wheat datasets, including genetic maps and chromosome-scale physical assemblies.


Current version is pre-loaded with the International Wheat Genome Sequencing Consortium (IWGSC) RefSeq v1.0 Chinese Spring assembly and other content of interest to wheat researchers, including:
  • Infinium 90k SNP chip marker locations
  • 26 90k genetic maps extracted from recent publications
  • Multiple marker sets for IWGSC RefSeq 1.0
  • Multiple chromosome-scale genome assemblies
    • Triticum aestivum
    • Triticum turgidum subsp. durum
    • Triticum dicoccoides
    • Triticum urartu
    • Aegilops tauschii
    • Hordeum vulgare
    • Brachypodium distachyon
    • Oryza sativa
    • Arabidopsis thaliana
Users can explore the above datasets as well as upload their own content.

A user guide is available here.

Please contact info@plantinformatics.io with any questions or queries.
